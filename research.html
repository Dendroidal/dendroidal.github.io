Research interests

My area of study is Algebraic Topology. Here is my Research Statement: link
In particular I am interested in Goodwillie calculus, the theory of operads and their Koszul duality, and moreover, the interplay between these subjects.
More recently, I have taken an interest in the theory of equivariant operads, and I am currently undertaking a major joint project with Peter Bonventre to study and connect different models for equivariant operads with norms.
